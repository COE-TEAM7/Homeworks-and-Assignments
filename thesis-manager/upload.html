Guidelines

Please be informed that the CSV (comma separated value) file should adhere strictly to the following rules:

  1. The csv should be separated by commas (',')
  2. The column headers are case sensitve and please, follow the naming scheme:
      University, College, Department, Year, Title, Abstract, Section, Adviser, Member 1, Member 2, Member 3, Member 4, Member 5
  3. The names of the persons in the should be in the order of first name and last name. Middle names are not yet implemented.
  4. Universities should be written in full. Colleges need only it's designation, i.e. Engineering for the College of Engineering
  5. Departments should be written in full, i.e. Computer Engineering
